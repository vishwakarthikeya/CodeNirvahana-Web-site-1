Welcome to TechnoFest

Annual Technical & Non-Technical Extravaganza

TechnoFest is an annual technical and non-technical event organized by students of Sri Nidhi College of Engineering. Students showcase their innovative ideas through various competitions, workshops, and presentations across all departments.

This year's Cybersecurity Department events focus on emerging threats, defense mechanisms, and ethical hacking challenges that push the boundaries of innovation.

Technical Events

Hackathons, CTF Challenges, Code Wars, Security Workshops, Project Exhibitions

Non-Technical Events

Technical Paper Presentations, Quiz Competitions, Debate, Poster Making, Gaming Tournaments
